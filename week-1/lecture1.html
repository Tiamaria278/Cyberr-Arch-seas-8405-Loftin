  • DCF: Linear, misses network effects.
  • Replacement Cost: Static, ignores freshness.
  • "Current methods are insufficient." (Baskerville & Siponen, 2002)

New Metrics Needed

  • Network Effects (Metcalfe’s Law)
  • Data Decay/Freshness
  • Intrinsic Value (e.g., trade secrets)
